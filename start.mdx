## Getting Started with HynixCloud

### 1. Create an Account

Sign up to access the HynixCloud dashboard. Account creation takes only a few minutes and provides immediate access to the platform.

---

### 2. Complete KYC Verification

**Mandatory for account activation**

To ensure platform security and regulatory compliance, users are required to complete a **Know Your Customer (KYC)** verification after signup.

KYC typically includes:

- Basic business or individual details
- Identity verification documents
- Contact and billing verification

Once submitted, verification is reviewed promptly. Services become fully available after successful KYC approval.

---

### 3. Choose Your Services

Select from a range of cloud services including virtual machines and hosting solutions. Resources can be configured according to workload requirements without unnecessary complexity.

---

### 4. Add Funds

Add balance to your account using flexible payment options. Billing is transparent, with full visibility into usage and costs.

---

### 5. Deploy and Start

After setup is complete, deploy your infrastructure instantly. Your resources are available immediately and ready for production use.
